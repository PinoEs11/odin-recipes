Easy and simple Pasta with Tuna

Are you a broke student? Try this pasta is easy , nutricious and cheap

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of pre-cooked pasta
  • Salt
  • Mayonnaise
  • 1 can of tuna

Preparation

  1. In a medium pot , put 2 lts of water to boil , once the water is boiling add the salt
  2. Add the pasta and let it cook for 8-10 minutes
  3. Once the pasta is cooked , stir the water out
  4. Add Mayonnaise
